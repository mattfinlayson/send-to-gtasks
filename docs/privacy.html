Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Data Collection

Send to Google Tasks does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Period.

What This Extension Does

This extension captures the title and URL of your currently active browser tab and saves it to your Google Tasks account. All data remains local to your browser until you choose to save it to Google Tasks.

Third-Party Services

This extension uses the Google Tasks API, which requires Google account authentication. Your use of Google Tasks is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

Permission Purpose
activeTab Read the current page title and URL
storage Save your task list preference locally
identity Authenticate with your Google account
alarms Clear notification badges after a few seconds
